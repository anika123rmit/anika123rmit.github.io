Crafting 4 Fun

  • Handheld Windmill Tutorial - toddler safe and easy to make!

Instructions:

  1. Using the ruler, cut out a 12.5 cm foam piece.
  2. Fold the paper in half to form a triangle.
  3. Repeat step 2 by turning the paper in the opposite direction.
  4. Using a pen, draw a hole in the middle of the paper folds.
  5. Use the hole puncher to make a hole in the centre.
  6. Use a hole puncher to create holes along creases.
  7. Fold every second paper slit in the middle to meet the hole.
  8. Cut 5-6 rounds from the colourful foam sheets.
  9. Use the hole puncher to make a hole in the centre of the circles.
  10. Wrap a pipe cleaner around a straw.
  11. Insert one of the circles through the straw and push all the way to the pipe cleaner.
  12. Insert the pipe cleaner through the back hole in the main foam piece, ensuring the straw is facing up.
  13. Add another cut-out circle on top of the pipe cleaner from the front.
  14. To shorten the pipe cleaner, cut a small portion off with scissors.
  15. Wrap a pipe cleaner around like a snail.
  16. Blow wind into the windmill using your mouth and watch it whirl!

Special tip: What to do if the windmill isn't working?

Use your hands to create a little gap between the windmill and the pipe. Push them apart slightly. Then, try blowing wind at the windmill again.
