Interactive Experience Research

Website- Palat Tuntaopas Portfolio (palat2023.com)

1. What was the first thing you paid attention to when interacting with the experience?

I noticed a number of visual components that were brought to my attention as soon as I opened the website. The occurring motif of colour changes caught my attention, along with the lopsided distribution of colours, which changed dynamically as I navigated the page with my computer mouse. The website felt dynamic and alive to me because of this interactive effect, which elevated a basic browsing experience to me that was extremely captivating and immersive. I was inspired to look into it further and engage with the website more because of the colourful aspect that the colours brought.

2. Spend two minutes with the experience and create a list of each of your discrete actions.

I started by placing the computer mouse in the middle of the page and watching how the colours changed in response to small movements. I then noted the changing colours as I moved the cursor around the page's corners in no specific order. Next, I hovered over the bold outlined words in the sentence, which drew my attention to textual changes. I also noticed that the colours changed from orange to purple, which made the experience even more exciting. Finally, I experimented with diagonal and vertical mouse movements, further seeing how the interactive parts reacted to different computer mouse movements.
